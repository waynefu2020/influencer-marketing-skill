# 建联邮件模板

## 模板 A：通用首次建联

```
Subject: Collab from [品牌名] x [博主名字] 🎉

Hi [博主名字],

I've been following your work for a while (really enjoyed your recent take on [博主最近的一个具体内容]).

I'm [你的名字], part of the [职位] team at [公司名称]. We are launching a new [产品核心词] that solves the [产品解决的具体痛点] issue many [目标用户] face.

To be honest, our tech is about 6-12 months ahead of the current market standard, and we are looking for a few "Pro-level" creators to test it out and give us honest feedback.

No strings attached. We just want to get this into the hands of people who know their stuff.

Also, if we love the content you create, we'd be happy to put our marketing budget behind it to maximize exposure (our past campaigns generated [X]M+ views).

Let me know if you'd be interested in trying it out!

Cheers,
[你的名字]
```
