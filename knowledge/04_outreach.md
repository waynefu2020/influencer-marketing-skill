# 建联话术

## 核心思想

> "Why should they care?" — 博主为什么要在百忙之中抽空看你的邮件？

回复率低不完全是你的问题。博主可能在忙、意念回复了、很多人找他合作、度假了……做到尽人事，听天命。把邮件质量提升上去，做到能力范围内最高的回复率即可。

---

## 1. 邮件结构四步法

### Step 1：标题直击重点

简短明了，表达来意。**切忌把 Collaboration 写成 Cooperation！**

推荐格式：
- `Collab from [品牌名]` — 如 `Collab from Apple`
- `Collaboration Inquiry: [Company Name] x [Creator Name] (Love your [Category] content!)`

### Step 2：友好自我介绍 + 产品亮点

开场白问候：
- `I hope this message finds you well!`
- `Hope you are doing well`

自报家门：名字、公司、产品。产品介绍一句话搞定，不要长篇大论。简单说明产品亮点（用户量、销量、背书），让红人觉得靠谱。

示例：
```
Hi [博主名字],
I'm [你的名字], [职位] at [公司名称]. We specialize in creating premium [产品类型] 
designed for [目标用户].
```

### Step 3：建立联系 + 夸夸

说明博主内容和你产品的联系点，展示你看过他们的内容：

```
We absolutely loved your recent post about [具体内容]. 
It perfectly aligns with the philosophy behind our product line.
```

夸夸参考：
```
We believe that your talent and charm can shine even brighter on TikTok, 
and we are excited to be part of that journey.
```

或直接说免费寄样：
```
Our [product name] is formulated with [unique ingredient] specifically to address 
[targeted concern]. We'd love to send you a sample to see how it complements 
your [相关领域] routine.
```

### Step 4：引诱回复的钩子

阐述合作红人能获得的收益：
```
Based on your content, I think our audience would love to hear more from you. 
We worked with many creators worldwide and brought them more reach and followers. 
Some of our videos even hit over 2 million views on TikTok.
```

结尾：
- `Let me know your thoughts!`
- `Let me know if you are interested in this project!`

## 2. 完整邮件模板

```
标题：Collaboration Inquiry: [Company Name] x [Creator Name] (Love your [Category] content!)

Hi [红人名字],

I've been following your work for a while (really enjoyed your recent take on [红人最近的一个具体内容]).

I'm [名字], part of the product team at [公司名称]. We are launching a new [产品核心词] 
that solves the [产品解决的具体痛点] issue many [目标用户] face.

To be honest, our tech is about 6-12 months ahead of the current US market standard, 
and we are looking for a few "Pro-level" creators to test it out and give us honest feedback.

No strings attached. We just want to get this into the hands of people who know their stuff.

Also, if we love the content you create, we'd be happy to put our marketing budget behind it 
to maximize exposure (our past campaigns generated 2M+ views).

Let me know if you'd be interested in trying it out!

Cheers,
[你的名字]
```

## 3. 回复率优化策略

### 3.1 二次跟进渠道

- Instagram DM、TikTok 私信发送友好提醒
- 联系博主的经纪人或商务邮箱
- 评论区留言互动，让博主关注到你

### 3.2 跟进时提出合作形式

**免费试用**（降低心理门槛）：
```
We'd love to send you our [product name] for free—no strings attached—to see 
if it fits your routine.
```

**付费合作**（博主对商业机会更有兴趣）：
```
We're offering $XXX for a dedicated post featuring our product. 
Let us know your rate card as well!
```

**独特激励**：粉丝福利活动、限量版产品等。

**成功案例引诱**：
```
We recently partnered with [similar influencer] on a successful campaign 
and think you'd be a perfect fit for something similar.
```

### 3.3 放弃原则

如果一个博主多次联系不回复，就换一个，不需要太纠结于一个博主的得失。有时候不一定是你的问题。
